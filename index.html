Member Rules

Familiarize yourself with Member Rules.

All Members must behave in a Mature manner. This includes;

  1. No swearing or inappropriate language
  2. No sexually explicit subjects
  3. Do not discriminate based on age, gender or racial background
  4. Act in a respectful and polite manner
  5. Act with maturity
  6. Do not ask for Promotions
  7. No threats shall be tolerated (Death Threats, ddos, dox, etc).

Respect others

  1. No bullying/harassment
  2. Do not slander on staff or members

Content

  1. Do not post inappropriate link(s) or images/videos
  2. Do not make inappropriate posts/threads
  3. No advertising/Server recruitment

Discord and Forums

  1. If you see or encounter a problem, screenshot it and report it
  2. We expect you to remain active, within reason, to keep up with the community.

Minecraft Server General

  1. Listen to staff directions
  2. Do not troll, harass, or bully other players in any way.
  3. Do not use an offensive skin, username or nickname.
  4. Do not reveal Real Life information, especially home addresses.
  5. Do not break the Mojang Terms of Services or engage in illegal actions.
  6. Do not scam other players either on the Forums or in-Game.
  7. Do not invite players to factions just to steal from them.
  8. Do not steal skins from players or maliciously edit them.
  9. If you have a problem with Staff or Players, report them on the Forum.
  10. Do not cause drama in public chats. Divert your complaints or reports to the Forums.

Minecraft Server Grieving

  1. Do not destroy other people’s builds
  2. Do not grief or intentionally make someone else’s build look ugly.
  3. Do not spawn camp
  4. Do not use a glitch to your advantage, and report any glitches you find.
  5. Do not use Cheats or Hacked Clients to your advantage, and report those who do.
  6. Do not use Auto-clickers, Macros or Mouse/Keyboard features to gain an advantage.
  7. Do not create traps involving portals or invisible portals to capture or kill players.

These Rules/Guidelines are subject to change. So please reread them periodically to ensure you are up-to-date.

Team Rules

Familiarize yourself with Staff Rules.
Dear Member: If you see one of our team members break any of these rules please report it to the owners and they will take care of it accordingly.

All staff must behave in a professional manner. This includes;

  1. No excessive swearing or excessive inappropriate language
  2. No sexually explicit subjects
  3. Do not discriminate based on age, gender or racial background
  4. Act in a respectful and polite manner
  5. Act with maturity
  6. Do not ask for Promotions
  7. As a staff member you are prohibited to; Reveal valuable information* about the server to the public or other servers. *Information such as Plugins, Staff chat logs, IP addresses, Control Panel access etc.
  8. Don't abuse your staff privileges.
  9. Obey your superiors

Respect others

  1. No bullying/harassment
  2. Do not slander on staff or members

Content

  1. Do not post inappropriate link(s) or images/videos.
  2. Do not make inappropriate posts/threads
  3. No advertising/Server recruitment

Discord and Forums

  1. Do not delete any staff members OR members post from the forums without reason
  2. If you see or encounter a problem, screenshot it and report it
  3. We expect you to remain active, within reason, to keep up with the community.
  4. Punishments shall be documented in the player report/punishments section on the forum.
  5. Punishments shall follow the punishments/rulebreaking guidelines.

Minecraft Servers

  1. Do not abuse staff abilities to assist yourself or others
  2. Do not engage in PVP using staff abilities i.e. /god, /fly, /tp, /vanish, etc
  3. Do not abuse staff abilities to impact the server economy
  4. Please, communicate with each other, use the forums
  5. Use the “!warn" system to take care of Rule Breakers

These Rules/Guidelines are subject to change. So please reread them periodically to ensure you are up-to-date.
